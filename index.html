About Me

I'm a Machine Learning Engineer and Data Scientist with three years of ML and analytical work on compliance-critical datasets.

Currently completing my MSc in Machine Learning & Deep Learning at the University of Strathclyde, I independently built FinWatch — a production ML system scoring bank customers for financial vulnerability under FCA Consumer Duty. I also led a 52-person cross-functional team to deliver a computer vision system deployed on Android for Kenyan farmers.

My work spans production ML, responsible AI, NLP, and computer vision — from Singapore government data pipelines to fairness-audited credit risk models. I care about building systems that are not only accurate but equitable and auditable.
